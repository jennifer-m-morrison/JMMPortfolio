My Story

Hi! I'm Jennifer!

Fueled by laptops and lattes

A proud mom of three, I began exploring new interests and career avenues when my children started graduating high school and leaving the nest. I have been homeschooling for the past thirteen years and wanted to discover my “second act” now that our family was entering a new phase.

When my son enrolled in a web programming class at a local community college, I thought his projects seemed fun and challenging, and designing layouts brought me back to my old yearbook editor days in high school. The next semester I headed over to the admissions office with my son, took a leap, and began my own journey.
